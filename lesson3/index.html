ForcastSquare

The Weather in Town

What's the point?

ForcastSquare is an interactive hub for town information. The purpose of this site is to be a hub for locals to find current weather reports and activities, as well as official town updates, and a spotlight on local businesses updated monthly. This website will boost town moral and encourage community activities, keeping everyone up to date on available activities as impacted by the weather.
