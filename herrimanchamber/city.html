Herriman City

Historical Herriman

In the beginning Herriman was actually known as Butterfield, named after Thomas Butterfield who originally settled in the area in 1851. A few years later around 1858, the town was renamed Herriman after a prominent town member named Henry Harriman. Even though people have been living in Herriman for over 100 year, it wasn't incorperated as a city until 1999.
