Fourth of July Fish Fest

The Fourth of July Fish Fest is a beloved tradition in the sleepy little town of Fish Haven. Many people, companies, and families spend the whole year preparing for it and others come from far and wide just to experience it. This wonderful festitval falls always on the weekend before the fourth of July and starts Thursday afternoon with a nice little flag ceremony ending on Saturday night with a bang at our spectacular firework show! Many of our local businesses even close early throughout the festivites so that the whole town can come and participate. Come join us and experience the history, culture, talents, and amazing food of Fish Haven. We promise it is an event that you won't want to miss.
