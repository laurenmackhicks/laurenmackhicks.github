3 Fun Things To Do With Your Family This Summer!

It's Summer time and family is home! Having all this extra free time can be a total blast, the days are longer and definitely hotter! After a few weeks though, the novelty of freedom might start to wear on you when you start to worry about how to keep everyone entertained. Well, fret no more! We went ahead and compiled a list of 3 really fun things to do with the whole family this Summer!

  1. Make a Playlist and Drive the Pioneer Historic Byway
  2. Pack a picnic and your swimsuit and go enjoy Preston Park
  3. Pull on your boots and go join the fun at That Famous Night in Preston Rodeo and all the festivities to be found there.
At the end of the day, there actually is a lot of fun to be had over the summer in Preston, get out there and enjoy all of it!
